About Us

Contact Information

This isn't pharmacy that happens to be in your neighborhood. It's a pharmacy that exists because of it.

Playa Pharmacy is an award-winning, family-owned pharmacy in the hear of Playa de Rey - Where personalized care isn't a promise on a poster. It's what happens every time you walk through the door.

Named "Best Pharmacy of 2025" by The Argonaut

Playa del Rey's own community newspaper recognized what our neighbors have known for years — that a pharmacy built on genuine care, not throughput metrics, still matters. Deeply. We're honored by this recognition, and more committed than ever to the standard it represents.

Being family-owned means something particular to us. It means the pharmacist filling your prescription genuinely cares whether it's right — not just technically correct, but right for you. It means we'll navigate your insurance so you don't have to sit on hold. It means when a medication is hard to source, we don't shrug — we do everything we can to find it, order it, and make sure it's in your hands.

It also means we pay attention to the things most pharmacies overlook. We see the young mother searching for something gentle enough for her newborn. The grandfather at the counter asking whether a new medication is really the right fit, trusting us enough to have that conversation. The neighbor who just wants an honest answer about whether a supplement is worth trying. They're the reason we're here.

On the Westside, there's no shortage of places to pick up a prescription. But there's a difference between filling an order and being filled with confidence that someone is truly looking out for you. That difference is what we built — and what we protect, every single day.

More than a place to fill prescriptions — a partner in the way you care for yourself.

Prescriptions & Everyday Care

The fundamentals, elevated

We fill prescriptions with speed and precision — but never at the expense of care. Every script receives our full attention, and we coordinate your refills through medication synchronization so everything is ready together, on your schedule, not ours. Transfers from other pharmacies are seamless. We send text reminders before each refill so nothing slips through the cracks and you're never caught off guard. We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicare Part D. And because your time matters, we offer complimentary same-day local delivery within our service area and free shipping on mail orders — because picking up your medication shouldn't feel like another errand.

Alternative & Holistic Wellness

For those who look beyond the conventional

We've always believed that good pharmacy means meeting people where they are — and for many of our neighbors, that includes a genuine interest in holistic and alternative approaches to wellness. That's why we carry a thoughtfully curated selection of homeopathic remedies, herbal and botanical supplements, essential oils, aromatherapy products, and naturopathic wellness items. We don't carry everything — we carry what we've vetted, what we trust, and what our community actually asks for. Our pharmacists are here to help you navigate these options alongside your conventional care, so you can make informed choices that feel right for your body and your values. Compounding

When precision means personal

Standard formulations work for most people — but not everyone. For patients who need an alternative dosage form, an allergy-sensitive compound, or a medication that's no longer commercially available, our licensed compounding specialists prepare customized formulations designed around your specific needs. Our lab uses professionally maintained, USP-standard equipment and sources ingredients from FDA-registered suppliers. The result is medication prepared with the highest standards of quality and care. This is pharmacy at its most intentional: every compound made with the same diligence we'd want for our own family.

Wellness & Guidance
Honest counsel, not a sales counter

Every over-the-counter product on our shelves has been carefully vetted — we carry what we believe in, and nothing we don't. But more importantly, our pharmacists are here to guide your choices. Managing diabetes? We provide dedicated education and ongoing support, so you never feel like you're navigating it alone. Unsure which pain reliever, allergy medication, or vitamin is actually right for you? We'll share what the evidence says, what fits your lifestyle, and what may not be worth your money. That's the kind of conversation that happens when your pharmacist actually knows your name, your history, and your health.
